Our Functions

With PillPal, you can manage your complicated medication schedule painlessly.

In-App Reminder

Our In-App Reminder can help elderly patients remember to take their medication on time, leading to improved medication adherence and better health outcomes. When it comes to managing copywriting for our In-App Reminder, we strike a balance between being informative and sounding friendly, while also using a tone that does not sound patronizing.

Sense of Personal Achievement

PillPal celebrates and congratulates you on every milestone through animated pop-ups and Points-Based Reward System. PillPal presents you with XPs after you have taken your medication.

As a result, PillPal is able to present you with a higher sense of achievement after you have achieved taking your medication as prescribed and encourage you to adhere to your medications perpetually.

Warning Against Recurring Risks

PillPal discourages you from missing your medication by providing you with several warnings and information about the health risks associated with skipping your medication before you decide to do so.

If you confirm that you want to skip a dose, PillPal can notify your caregiver and/or medical professional for additional support. We can alert them that you may need some additional support or reminders to take your medication.

Automatic Med Logging

Mobility issues can make it difficult for elderly patients to move around and perform a simple task, such as typing.

PillPal utilizes the camera to scan your medication labels, eliminating the need for you to manually record your medication intake. This can save you time and some trouble, and help you to record your medications more accurately.

Manual Med Logging

For elderly patients who may not have access to a camera or are not comfortable using them, having the option to manually log their medications can increase their independence and sense of control over their healthcare.

Manually logging their medications can serve as a memory aid, helping elderly patients keep track of what medications they are taking and when they need to take them, as well.

Predictive Search

PillPal incorporates a Predictive Search Function into its Manual Medication Logging. It analyzes each letter of your search query as you type it and generates a list of suggested queries in a drop-down menu. By providing suggested queries based on your partial input, PillPal can guide you towards the correct spelling, phrasing, or medication name you are looking for.
